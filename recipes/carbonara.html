Carbonara

Description

Get ready for the best damn dish of your life

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Parmeggiano
  • Spagetti

Steps

  1. Grab eggs
  2. Separate the yolks from the whites.
  3. Add the cheese to the yolks.
  4. Boil the pasta in the meantime obvs
  5. when "al dente" add the mix to the pasta
  6. boom enjoy you made your first carbonara you magnificent son of a gun
